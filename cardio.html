First aid for arret-cardiaque

steps :

1. Check consciousness:
Talk to the victim and gently shake them. If they do not respond, proceed to the next step.

2. Call for help:
Immediately dial 998 or 999 (firefighters). Request a defibrillator if available.
3. Check breathing:
Tilt the victim's head back and observe if their chest rises.

4. Start chest compressions:
Place your hands at the center of the chest and perform rapid compressions (100-120 per minute) until help arrives.
5. Follow the complete diagram:
Be cautious!!!.

Additional Tips

  • Stay calm and everything will be alright.
  • Call emergency services immediately.
  • Good luck.

First Aid for Dental Injuries

What to Do in Case of a Dental Injury?

1. Check the Condition of the Tooth

Examine if the tooth is broken, displaced, or knocked out. Use a light to thoroughly inspect the area.

2. Stop the Bleeding

Apply a sterile gauze or clean cloth with gentle pressure to stop the bleeding.

3. Preserve the Tooth

If the tooth is knocked out, immediately place it in a glass of milk or saline solution to keep it moist.

4. Visit a Dentist Quickly

Go to a dentist or an emergency room immediately for proper treatment. Acting quickly can save the tooth.

Additional Tips

  • Stay calm to reassure the injured person.
  • Avoid touching the root of the tooth if it is knocked out.
  • Do not rinse the tooth with tap water.
  • Use an ice pack on the affected area to reduce swelling.
